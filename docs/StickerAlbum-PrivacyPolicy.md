# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 27, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the **Sticker Album** app (the "App"), developed by Jesus Bohorquez ("I", "me", or "my"). I am committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

---

## 1. Information I Collect

### Data You Provide
The App does not ask you to create an account or provide any personal information.

### Data Collected Automatically
The App stores your sticker collection data (which stickers you have marked as owned or repeated) locally on your device using SwiftData. If you have iCloud enabled on your device, this data is synced to your private iCloud account via CloudKit.

**I never have access to your iCloud data.** Your collection is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to me or any third party.

### Data NOT Collected
- I do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.
- I do not collect location data.
- I do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
- I do not display ads.
- I do not share any data with third parties.

---

## 2. How Your Data Is Used

Your sticker collection data is used solely to display and sync your album progress across your devices. It is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than making the App work for you.

---

## 3. iCloud Sync

If you use iCloud, your collection data is synced through Apple's CloudKit service and stored in your private iCloud account. This sync is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). I have no access to this data at any time.

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → StickerAlbum**.

---

## 4. Data Retention

Your data is stored on your device and, if enabled, in your iCloud account. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the App from your device. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data. To remove data from iCloud, go to **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage**.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no features that require or encourage children to submit personal information.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. I encourage you to review this policy periodically.
