## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I mark a sticker as owned?**
Tap any sticker in your album to mark it as owned. It will turn green.

**How do I mark a sticker as repeated?**
Tap an owned (green) sticker again to mark it as repeated. It will turn yellow.

**How do I clear a sticker back to missing?**
Tap a repeated (yellow) sticker once more to clear it back to missing.

**How do I share my missing stickers with friends?**
Tap the share button (↑) in the top right corner of the My Album tab, then choose **Missing Stickers**. This copies a list of your missing sticker codes that you can send via WhatsApp, iMessage, or any other app.

**How do I share my repeated stickers?**
Tap the share button (↑) in the top right corner of the My Album tab, then choose **Repeated Stickers**.

**My collection disappeared after reinstalling the app. Can I recover it?**
If you had iCloud enabled, your collection should restore automatically within a few seconds of opening the app. Make sure you are signed in to the same iCloud account on your device.

**How do I enable iCloud sync?**
Go to **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud** and make sure Sticker Album is toggled on.

**How do I delete all my data?**
Delete the app from your device. To also remove data from iCloud, go to **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Sticker Album → Delete Data**.

**The app is showing the wrong sticker count. What should I do?**
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact me at the email below.
